Introduction to this game:
	Rule:
		You have only 30 second to eliminate the moving drums.
		You'll get 10 or 5 score if you eliminate one drum.
		The number of score you get depends on the timing you
		press the corresponding button to eliminate them.

	KeyBoard Function:
		Key-D or Key-K : press to eliminate Blue drum
		key-F or Key-J : press to eliminate Red drum
		Key-R : press to restart
		Key-Q : press to go back to menu
